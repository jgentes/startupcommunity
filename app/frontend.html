Our product and methodology

Build Clusters

Identify the leaders in your community and organize them around industry clusters. Each cluster includes startups, advisors, investors, and resources such as coworking space, incubators, and networking events.

Connect People

Startup founders can search for advisors and investors based on skills pulled from Linkedin profiles. Create invite-only networks for alumni of accelerators, universities, and trade organizations.

Measure Impact

Track your economic growth by numbers of startups, jobs, capital investment, and other metrics. View statistics at the city, county, or state level, or within a specific cluster or private network.
